## How to Install TiviMate on Firestick

### Method 1 — Via Downloader App (Recommended)

1. On your Firestick, go to **Settings** → **My Fire TV** → **Developer Options**
2. Enable **Apps from Unknown Sources** and **ADB Debugging**
3. Install the **Downloader** app from the Amazon Appstore (search "Downloader" by AFTVnews)
4. Open Downloader and enter: `tivimate.com`
5. Follow the on-screen instructions to install TiviMate
6. Once installed, open TiviMate from **Your Apps & Channels**

For IPTV Smarters Pro, no sideloading is needed:

## Optimising Firestick for IPTV Performance

These settings will give you the smoothest IPTV experience on Firestick:

### Clear Cache Regularly

Go to **Settings** → **Applications** → **Manage Installed Applications** → select TiviMate or IPTV Smarters → **Clear Cache**. Do this monthly to prevent app slowdowns.

### Use an Ethernet Adapter

The Amazon Ethernet Adapter (£14.99) connects your Firestick directly to your router. This eliminates Wi-Fi interference, the most common cause of IPTV buffering on Firestick.

### Increase TiviMate Buffer Size

In TiviMate: **Settings** → **Player** → **Buffer** → set to **Medium** (5 seconds). This pre-loads a few seconds of content, eliminating micro-freezes on slower connections.

### Disable Background Apps

Background apps consume RAM on Firestick. In **Settings** → **Applications**, force-stop anything not needed while watching IPTV. This frees up memory for smoother playback.

## Common IPTV Firestick Problems and Solutions

| Problem | Cause | Solution |
|---------|-------|----------|
| Buffering | Wi-Fi interference or weak signal | Use Ethernet adapter or move closer to router |
| App crashing | Low RAM | Clear cache, close background apps |
| EPG not loading | Slow connection or expired guide | Refresh EPG manually in app settings |
| Black screen | Codec mismatch | Switch video player in app settings |
| Channels missing | Playlist not refreshed | Force-refresh playlist in app settings |
