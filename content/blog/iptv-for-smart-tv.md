## How to Set Up IPTV on Your Smart TV — By Brand

### Samsung Smart TV (Tizen OS)

Samsung's Tizen platform requires a compatible IPTV app since TiviMate is not available on Tizen. The best options are:

**Option 1 — Smart IPTV (SIPTV)**
1. Open **Samsung App Store** on your TV
2. Search for **"Smart IPTV"** and install it
3. Note the MAC address shown on screen
4. Visit siptv.eu/mylist on a PC and enter the MAC address to upload your M3U URL
5. Restart Smart IPTV — your channels load automatically

**Option 2 — SSIPTV**
1. Install **SSIPTV** from Samsung App Store
2. Open → Settings → Playlists → Add new → Xtream Codes
3. Enter your Server URL, username, and password from IPTV Trends
4. Wait for channels to load

### LG Smart TV (webOS)

1. Open **LG Content Store**
2. Search for **"IPTV Smarters"** or **"GSE Smart IPTV"**
3. Install and open the app
4. Select **Add Xtream Codes** and enter your IPTV Trends credentials
5. Channels and EPG load automatically

### Sony / Philips / TCL Android TV

Android TV has the widest app support — TiviMate is available directly from Google Play:

1. Open **Google Play Store** on your Android TV
2. Search for **"TiviMate IPTV Player"** and install
3. Open TiviMate → **+** → Add Playlist → **Xtream Codes API**
4. Enter Server URL, username, and password
5. Your full channel list loads in 30–60 seconds

### Hisense VIDAA TV

1. Open **Hisense App Store**
2. Search for **"IPTV Smarters Pro"**
3. Install and configure with Xtream Codes login

## Setting Up EPG on Your Smart TV

EPG (Electronic Programme Guide) shows what's on every channel — past, present, and upcoming. To activate:

- **TiviMate:** Settings → Playlists → EPG Sources → paste the EPG URL from IPTV Trends
- **Smart IPTV:** Add your EPG URL in the playlist settings on siptv.eu
- **IPTV Smarters:** Settings → EPG → Add EPG URL
- **GSE Smart IPTV:** Settings → Playlist → EPG Url → paste and refresh

A full 7-day EPG guide loads for all 21,000+ channels within a few minutes.

## Troubleshooting IPTV on Smart TV

**Channels not loading:**
Verify your Xtream Codes credentials are entered correctly. Copy and paste from the message you received — don't retype manually. Check that your internet connection is active.

**Buffering during live sports:**
Test your internet speed (minimum 25 Mbps for 4K). Switch from Wi-Fi to Ethernet if possible. IPTV Trends' Anti-Freeze v10 Engine handles most buffering automatically, but a weak Wi-Fi signal can override it.

**EPG not showing:**
Refresh the EPG source in your app settings. EPG can take 3–5 minutes to fully load after first launch. Make sure the correct EPG URL was entered.
