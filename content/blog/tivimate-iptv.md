## How to Install TiviMate IPTV on Firestick

Installing **TiviMate** on Firestick requires enabling apps from unknown sources, then installing via Downloader. Here is the exact process:

### Step 1 — Enable Unknown Sources

1. Go to **Settings** on your Firestick
2. Navigate to **My Fire TV** → **Developer Options**
3. Enable **Apps from Unknown Sources** and **ADB Debugging**

### Step 2 — Install Downloader

1. From the Firestick home screen, search for **"Downloader"** in the app store
2. Install the official Downloader app by AFTVnews (orange icon)
3. Open Downloader and allow storage permissions

### Step 3 — Download TiviMate APK

1. Open Downloader and enter the TiviMate download URL (search for "TiviMate APK" on [tivimate.net](https://tivimate.net))
2. Download the APK file
3. Install it — tap **Install** when prompted

### Step 4 — Add Your IPTV Subscription

1. Open **TiviMate** and tap **Add Playlist**
2. Choose your login method:
   - **M3U URL** — paste the playlist link from your IPTV provider
   - **Xtream Codes** — enter server URL, username, and password
3. Tap **Next** — TiviMate will load all your channels automatically

### Step 5 — Set Up the EPG

1. Go to **Settings** → **Playlists** → select your playlist
2. Enter your **EPG URL** (provided by your IPTV service)
3. Set EPG refresh to every 24 hours
4. Tap **Update EPG Now** — your full TV guide will load in under 2 minutes

## TiviMate Configuration Tips for the Best Experience

Once installed, these settings make a significant difference to your **TiviMate IPTV** experience:

### Best Video Player Settings

TiviMate supports three video decoders:

- **Hardware (HW)** — uses your device's GPU; best for 4K and HDR content
- **Software (SW)** — uses CPU; useful as a fallback for problematic streams
- **ExoPlayer** — good balance for mixed SD/HD/4K channel lists

For Firestick 4K Max and Android TV boxes, set to **Hardware (HW)** and enable **HW+ acceleration** for the smoothest 4K streams.

### EPG Setup for UK Channels

For a complete UK TV guide in TiviMate:
1. Request your EPG URL from your IPTV provider
2. Set EPG refresh interval to 12 hours
3. Enable **Time Shift** if your provider supports it — this allows pausing and rewinding live TV

### Channel Groups & Favourites

TiviMate's group management is excellent:
- Hide groups you don't watch (foreign language channels you never use)
- Create a **Favourites** group with your top 20 channels
- Use **Recent Channels** for quick access to what you watch most
